Move back to the start of the document

At times you may wish to move the Phrase box to the beginning of the current document. To do this:

  1. Click on the Back to Start button in the Tools bar.

    Adapt It places the Phrase box in the beginning location of the document.

 Note

  • If the current bundle contains the beginning location, the Back to Start button is grayed out and is not usable, even though the Phrase box may not be in that location. You can use the scroll bar to get to the beginning of the document.
