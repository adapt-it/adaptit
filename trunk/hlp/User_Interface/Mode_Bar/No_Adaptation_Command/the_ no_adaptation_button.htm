The <no adaptation> button

This method is the most general and preferred technique to make no adaptation of a source text word or phrase.

  1. Click the Mode bar button called <no adaptation> . Adapt It clears the Phrase box and makes a special entry <no adaptation> into the knowledge base. There are two benefits to using this action.

    • In the document, when Adapt It moves the Phrase box forward, it skips locations where a <no adaptation> translation has been made. In most cases, this is the desired action.

    • Some words may or may not require a translation based on the context. In these circumstances, <no adaptation> can be stored in the knowledge base as one of the valid adaptations. So when Adapt It displays Choose Translation dialog, you can select <no adaptation> along with any of the alternate meanings.

 Note

  • No adaptation is one of several ways that Adapt It uses “nothing” to handle special conditions in translation. See Adapt It types of Nothing for more information.
