Remove filtered free translations

The Remove filtered free translation option of the Advanced menu allows you to remove all free translations from the document. When you choose this option, Adapt It displays a warning message:

  • Click Yes to remove all free translations from the document.

  • Click No to stop the process without removing any free translations.

 Warning

  • If you click Yes, all of the free translations you have entered are deleted and cannot be restored. You will have to reenter any free translations you want to use. See Free translation compose bar and navigation buttons for a way to remove individual free translations.
