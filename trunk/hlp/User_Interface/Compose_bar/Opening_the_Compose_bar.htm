Opening the Compose bar

Adapt It normally hides the Compose bar since it is not needed most of the time. You can open the Compose bar by:

  1. Open the View menu on the Menu bar.

    Adapt It opens the view options.

  2. Click the Compose Bar option.

    Adapt It places a check mark in front of the option and opens the Compose bar below the Mode bar.

 Note

  • To hide the Compose bar, click the Compose Bar option in the Edit menu.
