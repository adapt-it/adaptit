Interlinear text sample (with free and back translation)

This illustration shows both free translation and back translation formatted as separate table rows under the target text. By default back translations are formatted as footnotes. Here, however, the option has been set (using Export Filter/Options) to place back translations also in a separate row in the tables. Adapt It maintains the alignment of text elements by placing them in tables with cells of appropriate size.
