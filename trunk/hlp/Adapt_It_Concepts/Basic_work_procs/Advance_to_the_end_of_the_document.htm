Advance to the end of the document

At times you may wish to move the Phrase box to the end of the current document. To do this:

  1. Click on the Advance to End button in the Tools bar.

    Adapt It places the Phrase box in the last location of the document.

 Note

  • If the current bundle contains the last location, the Advance to End button is grayed out and is not usable, even though the Phrase box may not be in that location. You can use the scroll bar to move to the end.
